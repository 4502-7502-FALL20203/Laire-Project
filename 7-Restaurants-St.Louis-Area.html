Hi-Pointe

1033 McCausland Ave.
St. Louis, MO
63117
(314) 349-2720

Visit Hi-Pointe's website

Hi-Pointe has been an iconic St. Louis destination ever since it was a little drive-in in the 1980’s. The menu has something for folks of many walks of life from our creative burgers, sandwiches, and shakes, to healthy salads and a menu for the little ones. They have a wonderful atmosphere and even feature the restored “Good Burger” car at one of their locations.

Fitz's

6605 Delmar Blvd
St. Louis, MO
63130
(314) 726-9555

Visit Fitz's website

Fitz’s is a local St. Louis craft soda micro-brewery. They bottle 19 different flavors on their vintage bottling line on the floor of their restaurant in the Delmar Loop. They have great food and provide a unique dining experience.

Twisted Ranch

14 Maryland Plaza
St. Louis, MO
63108
(314) 833-3450

Visit Twisted Ranch's website

Everything on their menu is house-made, incorporating their very own ranch seasoning and ranch flavors in every item. They make 33 different ranch flavors fresh everyday. Their menu highlights a ranch flight that allows you to try each of the 33 ranch specialties.

Tuscano's Brazillian Grill

1520 S 5th St.
St. Charles, MO
63303
(636) 724-4499

Visit Tuscono's Brazillian Grill's website

The Brazilian tradition of grilling, or Churrasco, is a fusion of South American and European cultures. The food is cooked on skewers over open-flame grills and then the skewers are brought to your table where sizzling portions are sliced onto your plate. It is on the pricier side of this list but definitely worth it!

Drake's

900 Highway K
O'Fallon, MO
63366
(636) 228-3557

Visit Drake's website

Drake’s has a unique combination on their menu. They serve over 20 craft beers on tap, big burgers and the freshest sushi in town.

Pieces

1535 S 8th
St. Louis, MO
63104
(314) 230-5184

Visit Pieces' website

Pieces is a bar + casual dining venue featuring two walls of curated board games for play. Navigators are there to serve you beer, wine, cocktails, coffee, and food to your heart's content. There is a small fee but for sure worth it for the experience you get.

The Original Pancake House

1700 Chesterfield Airport Rd.
Chesterfield, MO
63005
(636) 536-4044

Visit The Original Pancake House's website

The original pancake house was created in 1953 and has been voted by James Beard as one of the nation’s “Ten Best” breakfast restaurants in the category of “American’s Regional Classic Award.” Though, the best part is their hot chocolate!
